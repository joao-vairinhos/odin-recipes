Sun Dried Tomato Pesto

Description

Also knowned as pesto rosso, sun dried tomato pesto is a paste made of sun dried tomato, basil and walnuts.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 large jar of sun dried tomatos
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 cup of basil
  • 1 1/2 cups of walnuts

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to the food processor
  2. Blend. It doesn't need to be completely smooth
